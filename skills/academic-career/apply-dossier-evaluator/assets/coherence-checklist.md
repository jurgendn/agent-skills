# Dossier coherence checklist

Line-items for Dimension 10 (dossier coherence). A coherent package tells **one
research story**: the documents corroborate, rather than merely coexist. Mark each
item Pass / Partial / Fail with a one-line note, then map the result onto the
Dimension 10 anchors in `references/rubric-descriptors.md`.

## Lead-narrative consistency
- [ ] The SOP's **lead narrative / research identity** appears **prominently** in
      the CV (not buried or absent).
- [ ] The CV's **strongest evidence** is actually used in the SOP narrative.
- [ ] All documents sound like the **same applicant** (one thesis, not several).

## Claim ↔ evidence
- [ ] **Every SOP claim is CV-backed** — each stated achievement maps to a CV entry.
- [ ] **No CV overclaim** — authorship/role/impact stated honestly (cross-check
      `failure-patterns.md`; venues rated via `venue-quality.md`).
- [ ] Letters can **corroborate** the SOP's headline claims (right recommenders for
      the claimed strengths).

## Target alignment
- [ ] The **research proposal / direction matches the target** program or sponsor
      (and, for scholarships, the mission — see `external-programs.md`).
- [ ] **Faculty/lab fit** in the SOP is specific and current (fetched live), not
      keyword-level or stale.
- [ ] For scholarships: the **mission / return-home** thread is present and
      evidenced, not bolted on.

## Weakness handling
- [ ] Any **transcript weakness / gap / anomaly** is **addressed** somewhere
      (briefly, factually) rather than left unexplained.
- [ ] Eligibility conditions (if any) are **acknowledged with a path**, consistent
      with the Dimension 1 verdict (`degree-equivalence.md`).

## Roll-up
- [ ] Net: does the package read as **one credible research story**? → set Dimension
      10 score (5 = one story; 3 = mostly aligned, loose ends; 1 = fragmented).
