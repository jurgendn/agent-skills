# Application components checklist (reference)

The standard pieces of a PhD/research application. Requirements vary by
program — always confirm against each school's official page and record
per-school specifics in `fit-notes/`. Use `apply-package-auditor` to check the
package as a whole before deadlines.

## Core components

- [ ] **Academic CV** — `cv/` (`apply-cv-builder`). Research-first ordering:
      publications, research experience, then education/teaching/awards.
- [ ] **Statement of Purpose** — `sop/sop-{school}.md` (`apply-sop-writer`),
      tailored per school; see `sop-structure.md`.
- [ ] **Personal History / Diversity statement** — where required; distinct from
      the SOP (background and perspective, not research plan).
- [ ] **Letters of recommendation** — usually 3; coordinate in `recommenders/`
      (`apply-recommendation-letter-strategist`). Confirm count and submission
      mechanism per school.
- [ ] **Transcripts** — official vs unofficial per stage; note GPA scale.
- [ ] **Test scores** — GRE (often optional/waived now), and English proficiency
      (TOEFL/IELTS) for international applicants; confirm minimums.
- [ ] **Writing sample / research paper** — where required.
- [ ] **Application form + fee** (and fee-waiver eligibility).

## Per-school tracking

For each school record in `_dashboard/schools-tracker.md`:

- Deadline (and abstract/early deadlines).
- Number of letters and how they're submitted.
- Required statements (SOP only, or SOP + personal history).
- Test-score requirements/waivers.
- Program-specific extras (e.g. faculty you must name, supplemental essays).

## Common omissions to catch

- Naming a professor who has **left** or **isn't taking students** — verify with
  `apply-research-direction-mapper`.
- SOP that doesn't connect *past work → future direction → this program's fit*.
- CV and SOP that disagree on dates, roles, or claims.
- Letters from people who can't speak to **research** ability.
